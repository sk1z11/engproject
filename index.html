How to keep a balance between virtual and real communication?

Time limits

Set specific time limits for using social networks and messengers. For example, set a time per day for online communication, and spend other time on real-life communication

Improve your interpersonal relationships

Use social networks to improve interpersonal relationships. Don't replace real communication with virtual communication totally

Get your priorities right

Take time to communicate with your family and friends in real life, also spend time on your hobbies, sports and other activities that help to keep your mind off the virtual

Events

Take part in different events where you can meet new people, make friends, and take your mind off the virtual world

Board games

Meet up with friends in real life and have board game evenings. So, by spending time together, you will be able to build stronger relationships with your friends and family
Remember!
Virtual communication is not a replacement for real life. Do not spend a lot of time in social networks, as real communication shows all the emotions and feelings of the person with which you are in dialog
